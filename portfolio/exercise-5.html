Exercise 5: Symmetry and Assymetry

These graphics were the result of my first time working with InDesign. I wanted to explore some of InDesign's capabilities while also completing the assignment, and fortunately InDesign proved to be a very useful tool for symmetry and asymmetry. There are many tools to structure the page that provide balance, or if you work against them as I did in the first image, imbalance.
