Exercise 11: Layers

Dropshadows. Yikes. What was I thinking. I had actually just read a design blog article about dropshadows and wanted to test them out, so there we go. It's not pretty, that's for sure. I was curious to explore having the bottom row of content as the longest, as I wanted to see if it detracts from the hierarchy of the design, and I do think it does. The way I've centered my div items on my portfolio page works much better I think. But the delineation between layers of content (boxes for each item, lines to indicate the next row, and of course those wonderful dropshadows to let you know that this is a 3D object) work well to offer different layers of content.
